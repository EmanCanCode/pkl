Our Story

The Premier Pickleball Platform

PKL.CLUB was founded with a simple mission: to grow the sport of pickleball by connecting players, operators, and sponsors on a single, powerful platform.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to making pickleball more accessible, competitive, and enjoyable for everyone. From recreational players to world-class competitors, PKL.CLUB provides the tools and community needed to elevate the game.

Our Vision

We envision a world where pickleball is recognized as a premier global sport, with organized competitions, professional athletes, and a thriving ecosystem of venues and sponsors supporting the community.

Tournament Platform

Comprehensive tournament management from registration to scoring, making it easy to organize and participate in events.

Global Community

Connect with players, operators, and sponsors from around the world. Build your network and grow the sport together.

Growing the Sport

Through events, partnerships, and community programs, we're committed to introducing pickleball to new players everywhere.
