  • 1. How do I enter the Pickleball World Series?
    Just create a player profile and purchase a PKL.CLUB membership (membership costs are prorated depending on the country you are in) then register in any activated city no rankings or qualifications required.
  • 2. Do I need to be a paid member to compete?
    There is a free membership option but to participate in PWS events you must be a paid member in good standing.
  • 3. What categories can I compete in?
    Your age and gender automatically display the available singles, doubles, and mixed categories during registration.
  • 4. How do I find an activated city near me?
    Use the PWS dashboard to search by country, region, or city and see which locations have been activated. If your city isn't activated help us find a city operator and get them to apply to activate any city or town.
  • 5. What happens if I win my city tournament?
    You advance to the regional event then national, and potentially the World Championship in November 2026.
  • 1. How do I activate my city for a tournament?
    Submit your operator profile and event details through the Operations Dashboard to get approval.
  • 2. Can I host multiple events in different regions?
    Yes, approved operators can manage multiple city, regional, and even national events.
  • 3. How do I register players for my event?
    Players register themselves, and their data automatically populates your dashboard for brackets and categories.
  • 4. What support do operators receive from PKL.CLUB?
    You'll get access to the PWS playbook, tech tools, and sponsor connections to simplify event management.
  • 5. How are results reported after my event?
    Results flow directly into the regional and national dashboards, ensuring seamless progression to the World Championship.
  • 1. Can I still join PKL.CLUB if I don't play competitively?
    Membership is open to all and links you to the worldwide pickleball community. You'll enjoy numerous benefits, along with exciting gamification tools powered by Web 3 that are on the way. This includes the Pickleball token, $PKL, for which early members will qualify to receive free tokens.
  • 2. How can I follow the Pickleball World Series?
    You can track live results, schedules, and updates directly on the PKL.CLUB dashboard. Also stay tuned for a global broadcast schedule.
  • 3. Do fans get any benefits from membership?
    Absolutely, members appreciate avatars, XP points, messaging, and exclusive access to community features. Whether you're a pickleball enthusiast or a casual player, our gamification elements will add excitement and enhance your pickleball experience.
  • 4. Can I attend events even if I'm not a player?
    Yes, we would be thrilled to have you join us in supporting these talented players at local, regional, national, and world events. These occasions are not just tournaments; they are celebrations that allow us to honor the incredible and dynamic sport of pickleball.
  • 5. How do I support my favorite players or teams?
    You can connect with their profiles, support them at events, and interact with them through the community tools available on PKL.CLUB. We also invite friends and fans to consider sponsoring local players, helping them with essentials such as training equipment and travel costs.
  • 1. How can my brand become an official sponsor of the Pickleball World Series?
    Submit Your Sponsorship Proposal through PKL.CLUB. Take the opportunity to apply for one of our global official sponsor spots, which will be featured on all branding at every event. Please note that inquiries about sponsoring specific or multiple events directly are not accepted. Additionally, we encourage you to support our incredible players in their journey to success.
  • 2. What visibility will my company get by sponsoring?
    Sponsors are featured across the PKL.CLUB platform, event dashboards, and live tournament coverage.
  • 3. Can sponsors support local or regional events instead of global ones?
    Yes, you can choose to back city, regional, national, or world events depending on your reach.
  • 4. How do sponsorships benefit the pickleball community?
    They provide resources, equipment, and support that make grassroots competition accessible worldwide.
  • 5. Are there opportunities for co-branding or product integration?
    Absolutely, sponsors can collaborate on merchandise, event activations, and digital campaigns tied to PKL.CLUB.
